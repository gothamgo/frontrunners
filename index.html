Frontrunners is a JavaScript and web developers conference held annually in the Washington, DC area. We feature a full day of interesting presentations from excellent speakers on a variety of topics of interest to web developers.

Diversity Scholarship

This year, we added Community Tickets to provide partial funding for our diversity scholarship program. These tickets are priced slightly higher than our normal tickets. But for each one sold, Frontrunners will also award a free ticket to one of our diversity scholarship applicants. Diversity scholarships are available to members of communities typically underrepresented in tech. Please consider purchasing a community ticket, instead of a normal ticket, and help us expand the tech community.

To apply for a diversity scholarship simply fill out an applicaton today!
