Video Adapters

The venue podiums have an HDMI cable for video projection. Please make sure your laptop has HDMI, or bring your HDMI video adapter. I have some adapters, USB-C to HDMI, for example. But please bring yours. If you need one and don't have it, let me know.

Audio Connector

We will have an 3.5MM laptop audio connector at the podium, if you need to share your laptop audio. You can also put our mic up to your laptop speaker. We will have lav mics and hand held mics.

Try the Projection and Audio Beforehand

Please try your presentation as soon as you can after you get there, either in the morning or a break. Make sure you have the mirroring, resolution, audio, etc. set up the way you want before your talk. It is much better to have those issues solved well before your talk begins! There will be power at the podium. The venue will be open to us starting at 7 AM. We will definately be there by 8 AM.

The projection is best at 16:9 aspect ratio. Also, please avoid small text on your slides. Bigger text will work better.

Wifi

Wifi will be available, and works well, but please do not depend upon it. Have a version of your presentation on your laptop, a PDF version perhaps. You will need to get a wireless account there, but it is pretty easy to set up. They instructions posted in the venue.
